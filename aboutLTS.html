Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United State?

The tortilla in these miner's tacos wasn't a hard U-shaped shell like we know today. Instead, it was a corn tortilla with a spicy filling. This daily staple was filling, delicious, and affordable. The Taco was first introduced to the United States in 1905.
